1. Edit Title: Click to edit title. Click again or hit Enter to save changes.

2. Minimize: Minimize the note.

3. Delete: Delete the note.

4. Add: Click or hit Enter to add an item from the input to your list.

5. Cross Out: Cross out an item.

6. Delete: Delete an item.

7. Edit Item: Click item to edit text.

8. Save Edit: Save your item edit.
